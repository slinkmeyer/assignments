This Week in Spoots Cat: Squirrels, and Why They Deserve to Die. Written by Spoots T Cat.

PHOENIX-AZ-August 2017

We've all seen it. Sitting in your yard, eating grass, rolling in dirt, throwing up grass. A smug bastard collecting nuts just "happens" to exist in that tree, and he won't even apologize for it. Over 12 million squirrels exist in the United States right now. That's 12 million squirrels who refuse to let us eat them, refuse to let hard working Catmericans disembowel them and 12 million too many. President Human has been soft, and it is up to us cats to take charge and destroy every last squirrel, after a brief nap of course. There was once a time when cats were a prized animal, instead we are the butt of internet jokes.
